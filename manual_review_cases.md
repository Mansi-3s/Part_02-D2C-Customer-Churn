# Summary

These manual review cases demonstrate that customer behavior cannot always be fully captured by automated segmentation. The review process highlighted customers with conflicting signals such as:

* High spending but low engagement
* Strong purchase history but support complaints
* Loyal behavior with increasing inactivity
* Dormant classification despite above-average revenue contribution

Manual review helps ensure that high-value customers receive appropriate retention interventions before churn occurs.
